RiverStatus Dashboard

Looking for an easy, at a glance view of the river safety conditions near your boathouse? The RiverStatus Dashboard augments your water safety plan with current condition data from sources including the USGS, NOAA, and the EPA for easy viewing at a glance.

History

The RiverStatus Dashboard and Safety Matrix was first created in 2018 by Max Garber for the Three Rivers Rowing Association in Pittsburgh, PA. Since then it has been expanded to allow it to be reused by any rowing club.
